Meet the team

Thomas (The Boss)

Thomas is the proud owner of Masters Barber Shop. He has been in the industry for over 30 years and has been working for himself since 1993 when he first opened his shop in New Ross. Thomas trained as a barber when he was 18 and has never looked back since. His friendly, bubbly personality makes your experience in the shop the best of the best. He is full of chat and takes pride in each cut that he creates. Thomas enjoys coming to work and getting to talk to his clients.

Marian (The Chatter)

Marian has been working with Thomas for years. They trained together at a young age and have been friends ever since. Marian loves to chat to her clients and is very interested in any topic that may arise. Marian has had years of experience working and dealing with people. She loves giving her clients a great cut for any special occasion that they are attending.

Gemma (The Blonde)

Gemma has been with Masters for 12 years. She loves her work and she is brilliant with dealing with children getting their first hair cut or children who may not be comfortable getting getting their hair cut. Gemma loves to chat to parents about what kind of cut they are looking for. She is able to deal with peek times when they shop may be busy and gives a great cut.

Richard (The Newbie)

Richard is the newest edition to our team but has years of experience. Richard joined us once he moved back to Ireland after living abroad for over 10 years working as a barber. Richard has been a great edition to our team here in the shop. He takes great pride in his cuts and really strives to give his clients what they want.
