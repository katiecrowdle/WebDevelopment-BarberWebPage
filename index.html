Welcome

Welcome to Masters Barber Shop! Thomas established his business in 1993 on John Street New Ross. He has over 30 years expereince in the industry and his team are also professionally trained with years of experience also.

At Masters you are guaranteed a professional cut with a great service from the moment you walk in. We currently have 4 members in our team who are passionate about their work that they produce everyday.

We are located in the town centre on John Street with a car park located ideally across the road.
